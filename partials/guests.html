Guest Speakers

  • Rachid Nougmanov

    Film Director

    Director of The Needle. Kazakh film director, screenwriter, and producer, former General Director of the Eurasia International Film Festival and President of the National Academy of Cinematographic Arts and Sciences of Kazakhstan, Chevalier of the Ordre des Arts et des Lettres of France. A pioneer of Kazakh new wave cinema, he connects Central Asian narratives with global audiences.

  • Kiki Miyake

    Executive Film Producer

    Producer and Founder of Little Magic Films, develops and produces internationally acclaimed film projects, such as the Academy Award-nominated ‘Cutie and the Boxer’. Bridging the East and the West, she acted as the US representative of Amuse Pictures, pre-buying English language films such as ‘Pulp Fiction’ and ‘The Pianist’ for distribution in Japan. In the music business, she represented YMO (Yellow Magic Orchestra), Sadao Watanabe, among other artists.

  • Joanna Stingray

    Singer, Actress, Music Producer

    American singer, producer, and writer who introduced Soviet rock to Western audiences, building cultural exchange through music and art between the United States, Russia, and Japan since the 1980s. Joanna will be joining us online.
