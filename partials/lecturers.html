Lecturers

Charlène Clonts

Associate Professor

Professor at the Faculty of Humanities, Department of Language and Literature, Kyushu University. Her research involves well-being through the arts, exploring how creative practices enhance mental health and cross-cultural understanding.

Kang Byoung Yoong

Associate Professor

Fiction writer, translator, and Professor of Korean culture and Literature at the University of Ljubljana. He studied in Seoul and Moscow, joined Iowa's 2022 International Writing Program (IWP), lives in Ljubljana and publishes novels in Korea, Russia and Brazil.

Heloísa Seratiuk Flores

Specially Appointed Assistant Professor

Assistant Professor at the Institute for Asian and Oceanian Studies at Kyushu University. Her background is in art and design, however, her interests permeate cultural and social factors, leading to her research into cultural expression in different countries.

Rieko Kamioka

Associate Professor

Professor at the Faculty of Liberal Arts at Chuo Gakuin University. She specializes in Soviet underground culture research and has served as a Russian language instructor for NHK's radio program “Back in the USSR” (Oct. 2024 - Mar. 2025).
