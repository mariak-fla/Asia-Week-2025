Soviet Film:

Establishing Connections Among Asia Through Viktor Tsoi's Work

Asia Week is a free, community-centered event dedicated to examining cultures across Asia, conducted in English and Japanese. This year's program celebrates Viktor Tsoi's international legacy across cinema, music, and art.

Special Guests

  • Joanna Stingray

    Singer, Actress, Music Producer

    American singer, producer, and writer who introduced Soviet rock to Western audiences, building cultural exchange through music and art between the United States, Russia, and Japan since the 1980s.

  • Rachid Nougmanov

    Film Director

    Director of The Needle. A Kazakh film director, screenwriter, and producer. A pioneer of Kazakh new wave cinema, he connects Central Asian narratives with global audiences.

  • Kiki Miyake

    Executive Film Producer

    Producer at Amuse Inc. and founder of Little Magic Films, develops internationally acclaimed projects that bridge Japanese cinema with global audiences, fostering cross-cultural storytelling and innovative film collaborations.
